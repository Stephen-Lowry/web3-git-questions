Question 2:

Compare CLI vs. GUI clients for working with Git


Answer:

CLI clients at a glance

>

Advantages of commandline clients

  • Very powerful and flexible
  • Cross-platform: works on all systems with no adjusting period
  • Learn once: cnce learnt, you don't have to learn new commands / procedures when you go another system
  • Can configure all parts of the program

Disadvantages of commandline clients


GUI clients at a glance

>

Advantages of GUI clients

  • Avoid having to learn off commands
  • Avoid having to type; point-and-click interface
  • Options are clearly and easily presented

Disadvantages of GUI clients

  • GUI clients differ from system to system and require an adjusting period
  • Not as powerful or flexible as their CLI counterparts
  • Not all options may be available and may not be able to configure all parts of the Git program
